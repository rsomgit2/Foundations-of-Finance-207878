Foundations of Finance

Course Description



The goal of this course is to develop a solid foundational understanding of the key concepts and methods in finance. This foundation is crucial in preparing you for your future finance classes. The course will cover the ideas behind time value of money, bond valuation, stock valuation, CAPM, risk and return, and market efficiency emphasizing both the intuition and the techniques. The videos will be followed by practice problems, which you are strongly encouraged to solve to check your understanding of the concepts. Basics of finance calculations will be demonstrated in Excel video tutorials. This course is strongly recommended for any student without prior finance background, and to anyone seeking a refresher on the core of their finance knowledge.
